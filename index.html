Profile

My name is Joshua Kirts and I'm a 17 year old automotive photographer living in Southern Californa along L.A. I'm also a programmer who makes software programs for MacOS, Windows, iOS, and Android. You can find more of my programming-related work here. It all started in 2015, where I recieved my phone, the OnePlus 2. Supposedly it had a great 13-megapixel image sensor camera, laser-auto focus, and a aperture of f/2.0 all made by a company known as OmniVision(OnePlus 2). As soon as I recieved this device, I began to not only start programming on it, but also start photographing unique things I would find in my daily life. As my passion for photography began to lift off, I began to seek a quest for finding my style of my photography.

  • OnePlus 2Click for more details

At the age of sixteen, I recieved my first camera, the Nikon CoolPix P500. This camera intrigued me. It was amazing at the time of how far I could zoom in and still have decent quality. I practiced with photographing nature and started editing with Google Snapseed.

  • Nikon CoolPix P500Click for more details

In the beginning of 2016, I recieved something amazing! The Nikon D5500 along with a 35mm Prime Lens along with the stock lens kit, 18 - 55mm. I immediately studied not only the camera's features(Aperture, ISO, Shutterspeed, etc.), but I started to get into using Adobe Lightroom as well as Adobe Photoshop. I started experimenting with different tricks in photoshop, lighting effects, and so on!

  • Nikon D5500Click for more details

One day, I decided to go to a car meet and I met up with Mike, an owner of a 2013 Mustang Boss 302. We decided to go to a numerous amount of locations to shoot his car. We ended up with some very nice photos and it was an awesome experience for a first photoshoot!
